# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind. Be honest. Help others create.

## The longer version

This project exists because someone needed it, built it, and decided
to share it. That's the spirit we want to keep.

**We welcome** anyone who wants to learn, build, contribute, or just
poke around the code. Experience level doesn't matter. Background
doesn't matter. What matters is showing up with good intentions.

**We expect** participants to:

- Treat others the way they'd want to be treated
- Give constructive feedback — say what could be better, not just what's wrong
- Accept that people do things differently, and that's fine
- Ask questions without fear of judgment

**We don't tolerate** harassment, personal attacks, trolling, or any
behavior that makes someone feel unwelcome. If it wouldn't be okay
to say in person, don't say it here.

## Enforcement

If something feels off, reach out to the maintainers. We'll handle
it quickly and privately.
